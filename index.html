--------TOP 10 TIPS-------

1- FIND SAVANNAHS!

Always try to find a better place to setup base and never where you spawn. Try to get to savannas which have many villages.

2- NEVER DIG DOWN!

As many times u can fall into lava and lose all your items and progress.

3- USE YOUR FIRST IRON FOR A SHIELD!

SHIELDS are very cheap as you can see above and play a very important role in protecting you at night againt mobs like CREEPERS, SKELETONS, ZOMBIES and so on.
